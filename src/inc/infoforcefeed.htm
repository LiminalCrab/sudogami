Infoforcefeed [website] is a small and tight nit community of developers located on the Freenode IRC Network. Though the community has an international presence, the majority of the members hail from Portland, Oregon. Unlike [Merveilles], Infoforcefeed has no barrier to entry, but it's abstract sense of humor functions as somewhat of a "culture check" for curious newcomers. Members tend to work on whatever they please, and argue over whether or not Preston should build a parser in Javascript.
