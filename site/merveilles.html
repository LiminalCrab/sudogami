"Merveilles is a community project aimed at the establishment of new ways of speaking, seeing and organizing information. — A culture that seeks augmentation through the arts of engineering & design.

We're trying to build a community that welcomes multidisciplinarity, with somewhat high standards of discourse, focusing on the topics of experimental art, music & code. We ask of our members to consider the impact of their works on the environment, and to optimize toward living more sustainably."

-- Devine Lu Linvega, Merveilles, XXIIVV, Arvelie: 12Z00

Merveilles began as an Internet Relay Chatroom on the Freenode network in 2009 and has since expanded into various other communications platforms with hundreds of members; however it's main hub is on Mastodon, located at merveilles.town.
